Build-out resumes at Loup Loup and win a custom TransAM from Transition

What a Spring!

  

Over the past few weeks Methow riders have enjoyed one of the largest blooms in recent memory and perfect dirt just about everywhere. Evergreen’s early-season push to cut out more than 200 trees at Sun Mountain ensured trails were open and running beautifully for all. A huge thanks to the volunteers who have contributed over 900 hours so far this spring. The Evergreen trail crew has now turned their attention to our Loup Loup North Summit project. Read on for details and come lend a hand!

  
  

Build-out at Loup Loup Continues

  

Late last fall, Evergreen opened the sixth new trail at the Loup, Oso Peligroso, to rave reviews. This season the Methow Chapter will keep up the pace and extend the Bear With Me climbing trail, add a traverse from Telemark to Oso, and construct a spicy new descending trail from Telemark back down to the base area. It’s ambitious and there’s even more to come.

  

But we can’t do any of this without your help. New trails require a major investment of both dollars and hours. Our Dig Days are particularly important this year as one of our grants requires a match of 1200 volunteer hours before the end of this season. That’s a big number but we’re confident our community is up for it. So come grab a shovel and do your part. This Saturday, May 31, we'll be hand finishing the new lower section of Bear With Me. Dig Days continue through the summer on alternating Saturdays and Wednesdays. Tools, lunch and a beverage provided; registration required.

Support Your Trails and Win a Custom Transition TransAM

  

This year you can make a contribution to the Methow Chapter and get a chance to win a new (and very shiny) bike. Thanks to the generous support of Transition, SRAM, Shimano, Wolf Tooth Components, Schwalbe and OneUp Components, we are raffling off a brand new, size medium Transition TransAM. The chrome frame is tricked out with a choice selection of parts and built by Joe Brown at Methow Cycle and Sport. Raffle tickets are $20, or six for $100, and are available at the bike shop and at Dig Days. We’ll draw a winner on June 21 so grab your tickets soon!

  

Celebrate the Solstice at Loup Loup

  

On Saturday, June 21, pedal-in the summer solstice, sample new trails and gather with the Methow riding community over burgers, brats and brews. Group rides will leave the Loup Loup base area at 3 p.m. and 5 p.m. to explore Oso Peligroso, Ursus, Bear With Me, Telemark, Fozzie Bear Inventions or Buckaroo. Then stay for a BBQ and the TransAM raffle drawing at 7 p.m. Kulshan Brewing will provide beverages. There’s no charge, but please RSVP.

Methow Trails Skills Park Opens

  

Over the last month our lead trail builder, Austin Graham (above), has been hard at work sculpting a labyrinth of berms, drops and tables at the Methow Trails campus near Horizon Flats. Thanks to his efforts, along with Evergreen's Jack Brown, Methow Trails staff and dozens of volunteers, the park is officially open. There’s something for everyone, whether you ride a strider or are looking to log air miles, so check it out!
